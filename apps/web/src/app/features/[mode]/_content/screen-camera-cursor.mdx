## How it works

Mode A composes your full screen, a picture-in-picture camera feed, and an amber cursor pulse into a single MP4 · H.264 (AAC) recording — all in the browser, downloaded to disk.

### Step 1: Choose the mode

Open [/record](/record) and select **Screen + Camera + Cursor** from the mode picker.

### Step 2: Grant permissions

The browser will prompt you for screen capture and camera access. Both are required for this mode. Your microphone is captured if available.

### Step 3: Record and download

Press record. The amber cursor pulse activates, tracking your pointer in real time. When you stop, the recording downloads automatically as an MP4 (H.264 + AAC) — or WebM/VP9 if H.264 is unavailable in your browser.

## Privacy

Recording runs entirely in your browser. No bytes are uploaded. No account required. The file goes directly to your Downloads folder.

## Codec output

`MP4 · H.264 (AAC)` — the browser's preferred format. Falls back to `WebM · VP9` if H.264 MediaRecorder is unavailable.

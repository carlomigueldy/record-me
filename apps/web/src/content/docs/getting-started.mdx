## Overview

record me is a browser-native recording instrument. No accounts, no upload, no install — everything runs in your browser and downloads directly to disk.

## Choose a mode

Open [/record](/record) and pick the recording mode that fits your work:

- **Screen + Camera + Cursor** — the full composition: screen capture with picture-in-picture camera and amber cursor pulses on every click.
- **Screen + Cursor** — screen capture with cursor highlights. Ideal for product walk-throughs.
- **Camera Only** — just your camera, round-framed. Perfect for a direct-to-camera take.

## Grant permissions

Your browser will ask for permission before recording begins. For screen modes, you choose which window or tab to share. For camera modes, you select the camera device. record me never stores these choices — every session starts fresh.

## Record and download

Press the **Record** button. A red indicator shows recording is active. When you press **Stop**, the recording downloads automatically — as `MP4 · H.264 (AAC)` where supported, or `WebM · VP9` as a fallback.

## Supported browsers

Chrome, Edge, Arc, and Chromium-based browsers support all three modes. Firefox and Safari have [limited MediaRecorder support](/docs/browser-support) — see the browser support guide for details.
